Igbo Language Text Samples for NLP

Example 1
Ndị mmadụ bịara ahịa n’ụtụtụ iji zụta nri.
(Translation: People came to the market in the morning to buy food.)

Example 2
Ụgbọ ala ahụ kwụsịrị n’akụkụ ụzọ.
(Translation: The vehicle stopped by the roadside.)

Example 3
Ụlọ akwụkwọ meghere n’elekere asatọ nke ụtụtụ.
(Translation: The school opened at eight in the morning.)

Example 4
Ezigbote mmiri ozuzo zoro n’abalị gara aga.
(Translation: Heavy rain fell last night.)
